My love for Animals

I grew up on a farm before coming to Canada at the age of 6. We had dogs, sheep, chickens; you name it. Our urban Toronto living did not allow for many pets so we always had budgies instead. Growing up, I always wanted to ride horses and have a dog. Fast forward 20 years later, I am living my dream.

My Passion

I have had a few professional office jobs in my 20’s and every couple of years I deep dive into a full-fledged lifestyle job with animals. The older you get the more you know yourself and cannot resist your true passion. I can tell you wholeheartedly I was born to take care of animals. No amount of office experience or University schooling could change that.

My Experience

I have over a decade worth of experience as a dog sitter and 3 years of professional dog walking. This has taught me how to read dogs, properly socialize them, keep them safe and most importantly it has taught me a great deal of love & patience. I am currently working on adding to my skill-set and can also provide dog training with basic things such as sit & leash manners to more advanced training such as recall, leave it, place & threshold/impulse control.

My Process

Upon receiving your intake form, you will be contacted to set up a meet & greet. Meeting up before starting walks is a great way to see if we are a good fit for each other. We will have a chance to discuss in detail your pups temperament, likes & dislikes as well as any preferences and protocols for pick ups and drop offs. Once we’ve set up a regular schedule, we are good to go!
